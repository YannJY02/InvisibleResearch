# Invisible Research

This glossary distinguishes exploratory research work from analyses authorized to support the paper.

## Language

**Exploratory Analysis**:
Analysis used to investigate data, methods, or hypotheses. Its outputs do not support a paper claim unless the analysis is later designated as Paper Analysis.

**Paper Analysis Candidate**:
An analysis that meets the project's reproducibility requirements and is eligible for review by the paper collaborators. Eligibility does not authorize its use in the paper.

**Paper Analysis**:
Analysis jointly designated by the researcher and paper collaborators to support the paper. Reproducibility is necessary but not sufficient for this designation.
_Avoid_: Formal Analysis, Final Analysis
