# Intake Inbox

This is the local-only, non-authoritative intake surface for unstandardized
research communications, papers, screenshots, and notes. Raw contents are
Git-ignored.

AI may read, hash, classify, and summarize new or changed items. It may not
move, rename, or delete originals without explicit approval, and it may not
infer a Designation Event from directory placement or intake content.

Only privacy-safe summaries, hashes, and stable references may enter a tracked
publication communication record.
